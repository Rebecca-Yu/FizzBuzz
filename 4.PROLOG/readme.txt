Purpose: Runs a 'game' of Fizz buzz 

Functionality: outputs the first 1000 FizzBuzz numbers

Fizz Buzz = word game where players take turns to count incrementally, 
	replacing any number divisible by 3 with "fizz", 
		any number divisible by 5 with "buzz", and
		any number divisible by 15 with "fizz buzz"

HOW TO COMPILE + RUN:
	make
	OR
	gprolog --consult-file fizzbuzz.pl
